Who Are We?
हम कौन हैं?
ॐ
SURYA ARADHANA

Ancient Solar Lineage

प्राचीन सूर्यवंशी परंपरा

The Sakaldwipi Brahmins — also known as Shakdwipi or Maga Brahmins — are an ancient community believed to have migrated from the mythical island of Shakdwipa, bringing with them a sacred tradition of Sun worship.

Revered as the original priests of Surya temples across India, they hold a unique place in Vedic civilization. Their contributions span Ayurveda, Jyotish, and the preservation of Sanskrit literature.

शाकद्वीपीय ब्राह्मण सूर्य मंदिरों के पुरोहित, आयुर्वेद व ज्योतिष के ज्ञाता तथा वैदिक परंपरा के संरक्षक रहे हैं।

Pillars of Tradition
परंपरा के स्तंभ
The Sakaldwipi community has preserved these sacred disciplines across millennia — a living heritage that guides our families today.
☀
Surya Aradhana
सूर्य आराधना
The sacred science of Sun worship — daily rituals, solar mantras, and the Surya Namaskar tradition passed down through generations.
🌿
Ayurveda
आयुर्वेद
Ancient medical wisdom rooted in solar and herbal sciences. Maga Brahmins are credited among the earliest Ayurvedic practitioners in India.
✦
Jyotish Shastra
ज्योतिष शास्त्र
Vedic astrology and astronomy — the celestial knowledge of nakshatras, planetary cycles, and muhurta calculation.
📜
Sanskrit & Vedas
संस्कृत एवं वेद
Custodians of sacred Sanskrit texts — the Surya Purana, Samaveda hymns, and oral traditions of Vedic chanting.
